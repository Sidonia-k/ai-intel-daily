# Financial Safety Policy

AI-related stock reports in this project are for research assistance only.

## Allowed

- Summarizing public market context.
- Listing research questions.
- Tracking business metrics, risk factors, and sector themes.
- Stating that content is not investment advice.
- Mentioning buy, sell, or hold in a negative-form disclaimer, such as stating that no buy, sell, or hold recommendations are provided.

## Not Allowed

- Providing deterministic buy, sell, hold, overweight, or trading recommendations.
- Presenting deterministic trading instructions.
- Claiming guaranteed returns or price outcomes.
- Using undisclosed private information.
- Hiding or weakening the investment-advice disclaimer.

Every stock-related report must clearly state: only for research assistance, not investment advice, and no buy, sell, or hold recommendations are provided.

Examples of prohibited deterministic advice include: 建议买入, 强烈买入, 应该买入, 建议卖出, 应该卖出, 建议持有, 可以重仓, 保证收益, 确定上涨.
